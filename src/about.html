Monochromatic

How it works

Install it. Pin the extension icon to your toolbar. Click the extension icon while viewing any page to toggle grayscale for that domain. The setting is saved and applied automatically on future visits. Repeat for all distracting sites, then unpin the extension icon to increase friction if you are tempted to see the colorful version again.

Why I built this extension

Part of the enshittification of the internet is design intended to keep us hooked and engaged. That is why features that fry our dopamine receptors, i.e., short-form TikTok-like content, cannot be turned off on most platforms.

The network effect makes deleting an account painful, and exercising self-control is not always sustainable. That is why I believe in slightly changing incentives.

After seeing promising results when I switched my mobile device to grayscale (an accessibility option), I decided to build a browser extension to try the same approach on my desktop. Give it a try.
